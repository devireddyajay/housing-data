Not Just a Number: Why Age & Space Matter in Housing

Overview

This Jupyter Notebook (not-just-a-number-why-age-space-matter-in-house.ipynb) explores the relationship between age, space, and housing trends. The analysis focuses on how different age groups prioritize space, the impact of demographics on housing demand, and potential correlations with market trends.

Contents

Introduction: Provides background information on why age and space are critical factors in housing.

Data Loading & Preprocessing: Loads datasets and performs necessary cleaning and transformations.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Visualizes key trends related to age and space in housing markets.

Statistical Analysis: Applies various statistical techniques to identify significant relationships.

Machine Learning Models (if applicable): Uses predictive models to analyze trends.

Conclusions & Insights: Summarizes key findings and suggests implications for housing policies and market strategies.

Requirements

To run this notebook, ensure you have the following dependencies installed:

pip install pandas numpy matplotlib seaborn scikit-learn

Usage

Open the notebook using Jupyter Notebook or Jupyter Lab:

jupyter notebook not-just-a-number-why-age-space-matter-in-house.ipynb

Execute the cells step by step to analyze the data.

Modify parameters as needed to explore different aspects of the dataset.
